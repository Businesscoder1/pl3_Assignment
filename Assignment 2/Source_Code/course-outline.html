Course Outline

  1. Introduction to Cybersecurity: Understanding the need for cybersecurity and its impact on the digital world.
  2. Threats and Vulnerabilities: Exploring various types of cyber threats, including malware, phishing, and social engineering.
  3. Network Security: Learning about firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and network defense strategies.
  4. Cryptography: Introduction to encryption, decryption, and secure communication techniques.
  5. Application Security: Understanding how to secure software applications and protect against common vulnerabilities.
  6. Incident Response and Recovery: Developing a plan to respond to and recover from cybersecurity incidents.
